cmlabs Plagiarism Checker: New Feature to Check Your Writing's Originality

Written by cmlabs, Aug 02 2023

Plagiarism has become a prevalent concern in the digital era, with the ease of access to vast amounts of information, making it increasingly challenging to identify instances of copied content. However, with cmlabs' Plagiarism Checker feature, we are ushering in a new era of content authenticity, providing users with the tools to detect and address plagiarism effectively.

To use our Plagiarism Checker, you first have to copy the URL of your website or your writing into the correct box box.

If you're using a URL, you first have to click the "Run" button for a validation check. After that you can click the "Run Copyscape" button on the right side of the page.

After a few seconds you'll get an overview regarding the plagiarism rate and also the uniqueness rate. The details regarding the text matches can be found below the overview section.

If you're using your writing, then you can simply click the "Run Copyscape" button and then get the same overview as if you're using a URL, but this time you can use the Word Density feature to help your writing needs.

For now this tools is only available for internal use within cmlabs. From the product standpoint, we're still at Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

If you have any questions or want to access this tool, let us know by email (business@cmlabs.co).
