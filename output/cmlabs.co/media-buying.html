What is a Backlink?

Just as the name suggests, a backlink is a reciprocal link that provides direction for users to move from one website to another based on recommendations.

In essence, backlinks act as "bridges" between two or more web pages, utilizing the aspect of referencing. It means that one website can recommend or refer users and direct them to visit another site.

Backlinks serve as a crucial marketing strategy within the internet ecosystem. In the realm of SEO optimization, they signal the search engines about the quality, authority, and relevance of a website.

If your website has many high-quality backlinks (premium backlinks) originating from numerous reputable and trustworthy sources, your website's authority will consequently increase.
