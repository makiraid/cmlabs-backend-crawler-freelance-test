Media Buying

Media Buying is split into 2 sub services which are Backlink and PR/Branding. Our backlink services are divided into two; Backlink to National Media and Backlink to Blogger.

Backlink to Indonesia Online Publisher is a service that helps your business improve its reputation through an article published on online media such as Detikcom, Liputan6, CNN, CNBC, Kompas, Tempo, Tirto.id, The Jakarta Post, iNews, and others.

This service can increase the brand awareness and visibility of your business by giving backlinks to credible sites. We can help you produce articles to be published, or help you get linked with Indonesian national media. Additionally, paid backlinks for each targeted keyword can be done when our team finds it difficult to increase the website ranking on SERP for a specific timeline.

Backlink to Blogger is a service that helps your business get a higher ranking on SERP through mentions on national blogs. But there are strict specifications on every placement, such as (a) website relevance or have relevant keyword to target, (b) domain age, (c) actively publishing article, (d) dont have negative keyword on the captured keyword (or at least under 3%), (e) commitment to publish our backlink above 12 months, (f) have domain authority that match with our specification, and (g) more or sometimes we need to add more specification regarding the backlink activity from our direct competitors.

At the site wide domain level, backlink quantity must be aligned with our direct competitor backlink activity. All the content that is produced for backlink activity, must be written by cmlabs.

PR/Branding is a service that helps your brand get mentioned in reputable media. It is intended to increase exposure through a relevant narrative for your brand according to the promotion needs.

At the certain level (high backlink activity), it is required to spread the placement into (a) bloggers, (b) High Quality Media, (c) Big Media, (d) Local Media, and (e) User Generated Content website, (f) Edu Website, (g) Gov Website, and more with very strict specifications and measurement on each activity with any external parties; at this stage, more budget is required and we ask for the understanding from anyone related regarding the impacts and results.

Pay attention to the following points regarding our Media Buying services:

  • cmlabs did not give guarantee that the backlink placement will directly impact the page ranking on any search results.
  • We try to be strict with this activity within our internal, but we cant ensure what may happen if the external team doesn't report the backlink activity to Google which can potentially make Google penalize the website. Were not responsible if the worst scenario happens.
  • This activity, at a certain level, requires us to spread the backlink to many bloggers, online publishers, and UGC websites to manipulate algorithms or to make our backlink activity as organic as possible. In this case, we may need more money for the placement.
