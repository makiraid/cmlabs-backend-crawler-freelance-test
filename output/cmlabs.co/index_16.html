What is cmlabs?

cmlabs is an SEO agency under PT CMLABS Indonesia Digital located in two big cities in Indonesia, Jakarta & Malang. We provide services and tools to support SEO activities and help companies, brands, or enterprises increase their brand visibility on search engines and the internet.

Our Values

  • Critical Thinking

    Our team is encouraged to question, analyze, and evaluate information, ensuring that our strategies and solutions are well-informed.

  • In-depth

    In every stage of our work, from ideation to execution, we ensure a deep and comprehensive process. We are committed to paying attention to details for optimal results.

  • Progressive

    We approach every initiative with a forward-thinking mindset to constantly innovate, evolve, and stay at the forefront of industry advancements.

  • Data Driven

    We consider data as the backbone of our strategies and decision-making process to ensure that every move we make is based on factual and valuable insights.

  • Analytical Thinking

    We approach every task, project, and challenge with a deep analytical thinking process to ensure that our results consistently meet the highest standards of the latest innovative opportunities.

Recruitment Stages

  • Application Review
    In this stage, we will assess your CV to evaluate whether or not you meet the basic qualifications and align with the company values.
  • Pre-assessment Test
    Within this test, we will assess specific skills, knowledge, technical proficiency, and suitability for the role you’re applying for.
  • HR Interview
    In this interview, our HR will examine your background, personality, capability, strengths, and weaknesses to determine whether you are the candidate we have been looking for.
  • User Interview
    In this stage, you will meet the user, who interacts closely with the position, to assess how well your technical skills and personality align with the team.
  • Final Interview & Offering
    This is the last step in our selection process to make a final determination. It's likely the last interview phase before you find out whether or not you will be getting the job offer.
