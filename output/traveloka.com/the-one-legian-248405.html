Frequently Asked Questions

What are available facilities at The ONE Legian?
There are top facilites at The ONE Legian such as AC, Restaurant, Swimming Pool, 24-Hour Front Desk, Parking, Elevator, WiFi. (some may require extra charges)
What is normal check-in & check-out time at The ONE Legian?
The standard check-in time at The ONE Legian is starting from From 15:00 while the latest check-out time is at Before 12:00
Does The ONE Legian provide breakfast?
Yes, The ONE Legian provides breakfast. However, you need to choose a room with breakfast or pay extra charge if you book a room without breakfast.
