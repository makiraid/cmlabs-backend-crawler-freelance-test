List of Attractions You Can Visit with Your Traveloka Kuala Lumpur Travel Pass

Here are some attractions and activities that you can enjoy with your Traveloka Kuala Lumpur Travel Pass:

Attractions

KL Tower

Includes:
1.
Entry to Sky Deck
2.
Entry to Sky Box
Operational Hours: Every day from 09:00-22:00
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Not Required

Aquaria KLCC

Includes:
1.
Weekday Entrance Ticket
2.
Weekend & Public Holidays Entrance Ticket
Operational Hours: Every day from 10:00-20:00
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Reservation is highly recommended to skip the long admission lines. Please visit the official website for reservation details.

Zoo Negara

Includes: Entrance Ticket
Operational Hours: Every day from 09:00-17:00
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Not Required

Berjaya Times Square Theme Park

Includes: Entrance Ticket
Operational Hours: Monday to Friday from 12:00-20:00. Saturday & Sunday from 11:00-20:00
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Not Required

Farm in the City

Includes: Entrance Ticket
Operational Hours: Monday, Wednesday, Thursday Friday from 10:00-18:00. Saturday & Sunday from 09:30-18:00. Closed every Tuesday, except on public and school holidays.
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Not Required

Museum of Illusions Kuala Lumpur

Includes: Entrance Ticket
Operational Hours: Every day from 10:00-22:00
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Not Required

Starship Galactica

Includes:
1.
Weekday Kids (Ages 1-16) Entrance Ticket
2.
Weekend/School & Public Holidays Kids (Ages 1-16) Entrance Ticket
Operational Hours: Monday to Thursday from 10:00-22:00. Friday from 10:00-22:30. Saturday & Sunday from 10:30-22:00
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Not Required

Activities

Jump Street Asia Trampoline Park

Includes: 2-Hour Jumping Session
Operational Hours: Tuesday - Thursday & Sunday from 10:00-18:30. Friday & Sunday from 10:00-20:40
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Not Required

Jungle Gym at Atria Shopping Gallery

Includes:
1.
Weekday Toddler Entrance Ticket
2.
Weekday Kids Entrance Ticket
3.
Weekend/School & Public Holidays Toddler Entrance Ticket
4.
Weekend/School & Public Holidays Kids Entrance Ticket
Operational Hours: Every day from 10:00-19:00
Redemption: Redeem at the attraction
Reservation: Not Required

Transports

KLIA Ekspres

Includes: 1-Way Ticket
Operational Hours: Opens for 24 hours every day
Redemption: Redeem at the station
Reservation: Not Required
