Description

No matter how far, Traveloka will always be by heart. Discover more than 20 travel products at the tip of your finger with Southeast Asia’s leading travel platform and unravel the world, your way!

Improve your travel experience with our user-friendly travel app, available in English, Indonesian, Malay, Thai, and Vietnamese.

Seamlessly book transportation, accommodation, or local attractions, and pay conveniently using Traveloka PayLater, credit cards, bank transfers, or other various payment methods.

Every day, Traveloka offers the best deal price for transportation and accommodation booking. It’s time to pack your bag and install the Traveloka app.

Do NOT book airport transport with them

I booked airport transfer from my home to the airport. Driver and travel operator was uncontactable at the supposed pick up date and time. Driver answered my call 15 mins later and said he was not aware of my booking and turned off the phone on me. I contacted Traveloka CS for refund immediately just to be replied with copy pasted messages multiple times saying that they are verifying the issue and it will take 30-60 minutes. There are no channel to call them directly so I can only do that via the chat msg within the app. Eventhough it’s clear that the driver did not arrive and pick me up at the agreed time for the ride to the airport, it still took them so long to verify and confirm the refund. In the end, I booked a grab car which arrived within 3 minutes. Traveloka CS called me almost 1 hour after my requested pick up time to tell me that the refund can be processed. Would definitely be late for my flight I waited for their process. Better order grab or taxi than relying on this app.
