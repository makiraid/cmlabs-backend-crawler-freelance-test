How To Book

Search your transport

Start the search by choosing your origin and destination, which must include at least one airport. Fill in also your pick-up date and time, as well as your number of passengers.

Choose your transport

Check out the available transportation options—public and private—and choose one that best suit your needs.

See transport details

Check the transport info, which details your pick-up and drop-off locations. Make sure to also provide your flight number if requested.

Fill in booking form

Fill in your contact and passenger details. Make sure they are correct to avoid any hurdle later on your journey.

Complete your payment

Recheck your booking before continuing to payment. Select your preferred payment method, and complete your payment.

Receive your voucher

Once your payment is verified, your airport transfer or airport transport voucher will be accessible via Traveloka App or sent to your email.
